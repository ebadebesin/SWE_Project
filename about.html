    • About


    The Wellness website is a platform to inspire, educate and empower natural healthy living. We wanted to create a haven for all things human. Share our passion and commitment to a kind, health and sustainable life

    Wellness is primarily being in good physical and mental health. Because mental health and physical health are so closely linked, problems in one area can impact the other. At the same time, improving your physical health can also benefit your mental health and other life domains, and vice versa. It is important to be aware that wellness is an intentional, ongoing and holistic approach to making healthy choices for in eight major areas of life functioning.



    In life, an average Human adult/ teenager needs an average of 7 - 9 hours of sleep per night and overall total wellness to be fully productive.

    Most importantly, students need complete rest to ensure productivity in school and their whole lives.

    Individuals need steps, tips, tricks, and activities to aid physical and mental well-being. As people want easy access to resources to help them improve their well-being, the developer provides this application as an easy, user-friendly way to access these tips, tricks, and activities.



    At the heart of our mission is the belief that everyone deserves the chance to live their best life. It all began with a spark of inspiration, fueled by the transformative power of nutrition and lifestyle choices. Now, we're a non-profit organization dedicated to sharing evidence-based health, nutrition, and wellness research and information with the world.
